ABOUT


Gaby Manurung is an aspiring graphic designer who loves singing, sunsets and surprises. She comes from Indonesia but have lived in Thailand for the majority of her childhood. Ever since she was little, Gaby loved The Arts. She would bounce about her house singing for her family and was only quiet when she concentrated on expressing her creativity through her drawings. Throughout the years, Gaby continued to grow her passion for The Arts by creating multiple artworks and performing in a variety of concerts.

This website is to share Gaby’s personal life and daily activities, as well as to showcase her works and projects.
